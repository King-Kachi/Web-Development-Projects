The Best Movies According to KingK

My top 3 movies (Animes) so far.

Koe no Katachi (A Silent Voice)

Were do I even begin, a truly heartfelt and touching movie. It is important to treat others with kindness and respect, and also learn to forgive not just others but ourselves.

Josee to Tora to Sakana-tachi (Josee, the Tiger and the Fish)

This is an epic and wonderful story. How far are you willing to go to achieve your dreams. Face your fears head on so you may overcome them.

Spirited Away

This is my favourite anime. I love the beautiful images.
